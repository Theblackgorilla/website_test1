Defining Characteristics of Classical Hollywood

Main Struggle to Solve a Goal

Movies were focused on resolving a clear main struggle.

Clear Victory or Defeat

Each movie ended with a clear resolution, often a victory or a defeat.

Conflict with External Circumstances

Characters often dealt with external challenges in their journey.

The Rise and Fall of the Golden Age

By the 1960s, the golden age of Hollywood was at an end. Actors retired, television became popular, and foreign films provided competition. In 1948, a Supreme Court ruling against the Big Five ended Hollywood's oligarchical reign.

1927 - Start of the Golden Age

1948 - Supreme Court ruling

1969 - End of the Golden Age

Alfred Hitchcock: Master of Suspense

Known for his groundbreaking psychological thrillers, Alfred Hitchcock directed some of the most iconic films of this era including *Psycho*, *Vertigo*, and *Rear Window*.
